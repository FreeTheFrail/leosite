Grassland Restoration Field Trip to Buffalo Beats

Sunday, June 9, 2019 at 2:30 - 5:30

The native meadow restoration work happening at Woodcock Nature Preserve is partially informed by a remnant prairie nearby. The prairie is called Buffalo Beats, and is on the Wayne near Nelsonville. Badger will lead a first exploratory hike there for interested friends of Woodcock Nature Preserve. Meet at Wayne National Forest headquarters parking lot to carpool. RSVP required.


Spring Bird Walk

Saturday, April 20, 2019 at 8 AM - 12 PM

Hi friends, We'd like to invite you for a little get together at Woodcock Nature Preserve, where our resident bird expert, Bob Placier, will lead us on a walk. Come out for a day of spring time sun and fun- with any luck we'll chance to observe a variety of birds. We'll be meeting at 8am in the meadow along the tree line- the dirt path (not gravel) from Marshfield rd at mailbox #5819. (5819 marshfield rd, new marshfield oh 45766 @ 8am) Please be prepared for weather, ticks, and walking, and bring your own binoculars and refreshments. All are invited- donations are appreciated. This event will be canceled in case of rain.
