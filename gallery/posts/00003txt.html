I’ve released an EP called Dark Waves. It’s pretty short, with a run time of about 13 minutes.

It’s the third “installation” (for lack of a better term) of my self-imposed 3 day EPs challenge, in which I produce a little musical project within a 3 day time period. I slightly cheated this time, as part of the fourth track had been written a few months ago. I felt that it fit the album though, so I allowed it.

Here’s a link to it on Bandcamp. You can buy it for any amount you want there, or you could just directly download it from the Downloads page. My two other EP’s are located there as well.

My favorite songs from this project are “Curl of the Wave” and “Soul Garden”.
